# Foundation

## 1. Purpose

Articulate exists to explore how AI-native systems should be designed, implemented, operated and evolved through a production-oriented reference architecture.

## 2. Vision

AI-native systems deserve their own architectural patterns rather than adaptations of traditional enterprise software.

## 3. Principles

- **Start with problems**: Technology is never the first decision.

- **Architecture before implementation**: Reasoning precedes coding.

- **Build evidence**: Claims should be supported by working software.

- **Document evolution**: Architectural changes are recorded, not hidden.

- **Prefer simplicity**: Complexity must justify itself.

- **Design for production**: Experiments should be capable of evolving into production-quality solutions.

## 5. Design Values

- Clarity over cleverness.
- Explicit over implicit.
- Observable over opaque.
- Evolution over perfection.
- Narrative over documentation.
- Production thinking from day one.
- Human collaboration remains central.

## 6. What Articulate Is

Articulate is:

- a production-oriented reference architecture.
- an architectural laboratory.
- a public engineering journal.
- a collection of architectural patterns.
- a body of evidence.
- a long-term research programme.

## 7. What Articulate Is Not

Articulate is not:

- another chatbot
- an LLM wrapper
- a framework demonstration
- a prompt library
- a benchmark project
- a technology showcase

## 8. How Decisions Are Made

When multiple solutions are viable, the one that best illustrates architectural reasoning is preferred.

## 9. Evolution

- Earlier decisions are expected to change.
- Superseded architectures are retained as historical evidence rather than hidden.
- Architectural evolution is part of the project rather than evidence of failure.

## 10. Success

Articulate is successful if it improves the understanding of AI-native architecture and demonstrates those ideas through production-oriented software.

# Prompt: Review Traceability

You are reviewing Articulate Journal production artefacts.

The written Markdown episode is canonical. The goal is to check whether derived production material preserves the author's meaning and remains traceable to the source.

Review the provided production plan, storyboard, narration script, scene list and asset register.

Check that:

- every important narration claim is grounded in the source episode or explicitly labelled as an editorial addition;
- every important visual concept is grounded in the source episode or explicitly labelled as an editorial addition;
- generated interpretation is distinguished from authored source material;
- uncertainty is identified rather than hidden;
- no unsupported dates, motivations, personal history, project facts or technical claims have been introduced;
- companion scenes do not imply autonomous authorship;
- human review gates are present.

Output:

- pass or fail;
- findings grouped by artefact;
- source references that appear weak or missing;
- recommended changes before approval;
- items that require human editorial judgement.

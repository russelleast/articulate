# Test Plan

## Objective

Validate whether the proof-of-concept artefacts are sufficient to produce and review a 60-90 second video slice.

## Tests

### T001 - Traceability Pass

Check each narration segment, scene and asset against its source reference.

Pass condition: no material claim lacks a source reference or editorial label.

### T002 - Companion Boundary Pass

Review opening and closing scenes for authorship clarity.

Pass condition: viewer can understand that Russell is the author and narrator, while the companion is an AI-generated visual presenter.

### T003 - Visual Medium Pass

Check whether the slice avoids becoming a continuous slide deck or continuous talking head.

Pass condition: includes companion opening, transition away, non-slide visual sequence, concept diagram and return to companion.

### T004 - Assembly Feasibility Pass

Attempt a rough timing assembly from the scene list and script.

Pass condition: target duration remains between 60 and 90 seconds without rushed narration.

### T005 - Review Readiness Pass

Confirm that generated or captured assets can be registered and reviewed.

Pass condition: every planned asset has an id, source, generation method, ownership note, version and review note.

## Outputs

- Completed scorecard.
- Updated findings.
- List of constraints to consider for future ADRs.
- Recommendation on whether to generate a rough cut.

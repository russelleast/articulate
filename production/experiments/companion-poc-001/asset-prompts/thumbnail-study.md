# Thumbnail Study Prompt

Purpose: explore whether the central question can become a thumbnail concept.

## Prompt

Create a restrained thumbnail concept for "Why Articulate Exists". Show a clear contrast between disconnected architecture documents and an emerging connected architectural knowledge model. Include the phrase "Why Articulate Exists" or the shorter question "Architecture as living knowledge?" Use a serious architectural journal tone, high readability, no exaggerated facial expression, no clickbait styling, no vendor logos.

## Variations

- Companion small and secondary, looking toward the knowledge model.
- No companion; only source fragments and the central question.
- Repository or journal page as subtle evidence in the background.

## Review Checks

- Does not imply a finished product.
- Does not overstate AI capability.
- Readable at small sizes.
- Consistent with the companion disclosure and source traceability.

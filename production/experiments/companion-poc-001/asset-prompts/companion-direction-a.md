# Companion Prompt - Direction A

Purpose: generate still-frame candidates for Direction A, Digital Architect.

## Prompt

Create a clearly stylised AI visual presenter for an architectural technology journal. The presenter is recognisably human-shaped but not photorealistic, with a calm technical presence, restrained expression, structured neutral clothing and subtle architectural linework in the visual treatment. The presenter should feel credible to senior software architects, not like a mascot, celebrity, cyberpunk character or corporate training avatar. Medium presenter framing, simple studio-like background with faint diagram cues, soft balanced lighting, room for title text on one side.

## Negative Guidance

Avoid photorealism, Russell likeness, celebrity likeness, exaggerated emotion, glossy corporate video styling, cyberpunk, neon, mascot features, lab coat, headset, futuristic armour, busy interface overlays.

## Review Checks

- Clearly artificial rather than deceptive.
- No author resemblance.
- Calm and technically literate.
- Can transition attention to diagrams.

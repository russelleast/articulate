# Companion Prompt - Direction C

Purpose: generate illustrated companion candidates for Direction C, Illustrated Presenter.

## Prompt

Create an illustrated visual presenter for a thoughtful architectural technology journal. The figure should be clearly artificial and editorial, drawn in a refined sketchbook or documentary illustration style, with calm expression, simple neutral clothing and subtle architectural diagram motifs nearby. The presenter should feel like a visual guide through source material and concept diagrams, not like a mascot or cartoon character. Medium framing, clean composition, warm neutral lighting, space for sparse on-screen text.

## Negative Guidance

Avoid photorealism, caricature, comic mascot style, exaggerated facial performance, cyberpunk, glossy corporate training aesthetics, childish illustration, dense background detail.

## Review Checks

- Low impersonation risk.
- Still credible for experienced technical viewers.
- Style can extend into diagrams and transitions.
- Can work without detailed lip-sync.

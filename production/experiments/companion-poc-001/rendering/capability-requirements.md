# Capability Requirements

## Required For This POC

- Generate or render at least one companion opening shot.
- Generate or render one companion closing shot with consistent appearance.
- Produce a non-slide visual sequence showing fragmented architecture knowledge.
- Produce one concept diagram or animated transformation.
- Assemble narration, visuals, basic titles and transitions into a reviewable 60-90 second rough cut.
- Retain source references, prompts, asset ids and review status.

## Required Qualities

- The companion must be clearly artificial and not a Russell likeness.
- Motion must be restrained and credible.
- Lip-sync, if used, must not distract from the argument.
- Text must be readable and sparse.
- Visuals must support source-grounded claims.
- The assembly must remain editable after review.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Permanent avatar vendor selection.
- Production-grade video platform selection.
- Automated publishing.
- Full Episode 0001 production.
- Digital clone of Russell.
- Continuous talking-head format.

## Constraints To Expose

- How much companion consistency is needed across scenes.
- Whether lip-sync quality is acceptable for thoughtful technical narration.
- Whether generated visuals preserve the source argument without adding claims.
- What asset metadata is needed before binary assets exist.
- Which assembly steps can stay manual during experimentation.
